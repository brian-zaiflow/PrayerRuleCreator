Personal Supplications

For Those Whom I Have Wronged
Lord, I pray to thee on behalf of all whom I, a sinner, have sorrowed, offended, or insulted, in word, deed, or thought, with knowledge or in ignorance: do not abandon them, but, in thy mercy, help them and save them.
For Those Who Grieve Me
I pray thee, Lord, especially for those who have offended me in any way, or have sorrowed me, or done me some other evil: do not punish them on account of me, a sinner, but instead pour out thy grace upon them.
Prayer for Mercy
Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on thy servant N., and through his (her) prayers, save me, a sinner.
To the Theotokos for Healing and Guidance
O most Holy Theotokos, look upon us with thy tender mercy. Heal the wounds of our hearts; grant us clarity of mind, humility, and understanding. If it is God’s will, open the way for reconciliation; but if not, give us peace, acceptance, and growth along our paths. Intercede for us before thy Son, that he may guide our hearts and bless our lives. Amen.
To Find a Spouse
O Lord of goodness and mercy, I know that my happiness depends on the love of thee with all my heart and soul. Guide my soul and fill my heart, O my Maker and God, for my desire is to please thee alone. Preserve me from pride, self-love, and laziness. Clothe me with reason, modesty, and chastity. And as thou didst bless the estate of marriage by thy presence in Cana, and commanded mankind to be fruitful and multiply, so also enable me to fulfill this holy calling; not to satisfy my desires, but to fulfill thy holy purpose. For thou hast said: It is not good for man to be alone, but thou madest a woman to help him. Hear my humble prayer from the depths of my heart, and grant me an honorable and godly spouse, that we may live together in love, with one mind, to thy glory, O compassionate God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, now and ever and unto ages of ages. Amen.
For Discernment
O Lord Jesus Christ our God, who didst lead the people of Israel in a land unsown, a land of drought and deep darkness, a land none may pass through or dwell in: do thou, the same Lord, lead me all the days of my life, giving me to discern the times and their signs, and to redeem time's passage—for the days are evil—guiding me in the way that I should go, that I may ever say: I love thee, O Lord, for thou hast heard the voice of my supplication from my mother's womb, yea, from all eternity, and thou art the Way and the Truth, and the Resurrection and the Life, the Sunshine that ripens all who live and repose in thee, the Peace of God, and also our Peace with him: always, now and ever and unto ages of ages. Amen.
